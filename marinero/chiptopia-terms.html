If you void a qualifying purchase credited toward achievement of a status level, Chipotle will deduct that purchase from the purchases credited toward the status level.

By registering for Chiptopia in July 2016, a participant’s Chiptopia card will automatically be credited with an additional Reward offer good for a free order of Chipotle chips & guac with the participant’s first qualifying purchase following registration.

For the Chiptopia Summer Rewards Program, there are three status levels within Chiptopia that are determined by the number of qualifying purchases in a calendar month during the Summer Rewards Program term (July, August and September of 2016). In each such calendar month, four qualifying purchases entitles a Chiptopia participant to “Mild Status” and a Reward consisting of one free Chipotle entrée item. After achievement of Mild Status, four additional qualifying purchases (one of which may consist of redemption of the Mild Status Reward entrée) entitles the participant to “Medium Status” and one additional Reward consisting of one free Chipotle entrée item. After achievement of Medium Status, three additional qualifying purchases (which may consist of redemption of the Mild Status and/or Medium Status Reward entrées) entitles the participant to “Hot Status” and one additional Reward consisting of one free Chipotle entrée item. After achievement of Hot Status in a particular month, additional purchases in that month will not count toward any further status levels or accrual of any additional Rewards.

Status levels that have been achieved in one calendar month in the Summer Rewards Program and the number of qualifying purchases that have been made in that month will be reset to zero at the end of the applicable calendar month, so at the beginning of the next month (i.e. at the beginning of both August and September), each Chiptopia participant will start making qualifying purchases without any previously-credited qualifying purchases.

Achievement of status levels over a given program period may entitle Chiptopia participants to additional Rewards we refer to as “Bonus Rewards”. During the Summer Rewards Program, if a participant achieves Mild Status in all three of the calendar months that are included in the Summer Rewards Program (i.e. achieving Mild Status in July, and then again in August and then again in September), the participant will be entitled to receive a Bonus Reward consisting of one free Chipotle entrée item; if a participant achieves Medium Status in all three of the calendar months that are included in the Summer Rewards Program, then in lieu of the Mild Status Bonus Reward, the participant will be entitled to receive a Bonus Reward consisting of a $20 credit redeemable through Chipotle’s online store to cover purchase costs, shipping and handling, and any applicable sales taxes; and if a participant achieve Hot Status in all three of the calendar months that are included in the Summer Rewards Program, then in lieu of the Mild Status Bonus Reward and the Medium Status Bonus Reward, the participant will be entitled to receive a Bonus Reward consisting of one free Chipotle Catering for 20 order (or Burritos by the Box for 20 order depending on local availability of catering). These Bonus Rewards are not cumulative and are limited to one Bonus Reward per participant; a participant will be entitled only to the Bonus Reward associated with the highest status level achieved by the participant in all three program months. Participants that have earned a Bonus Reward may not “trade down” to a lower level Bonus Reward.

Bonus Rewards earned, if any, will expire as follows: the Bonus Rewards for achieving at least Mild Status (i.e., the additional free entrée item) or at least Medium Status (i.e., the $20 online store credit) in all three calendar months that are included in the Summer Rewards Program will each expire on December 31, 2016; the Bonus Rewards for achieving Hot Status in all three calendar months (i.e., the Chipotle Catering for 20 order) will expire on March 31, 2017. To redeem a Chipotle Catering Bonus Reward, please order at least 7 days in advance for the best chance of our being able to accommodate your preferred date.
